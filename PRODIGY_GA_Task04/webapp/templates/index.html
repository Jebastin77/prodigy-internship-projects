How the verdict is drawn

  1. 01Source image is resized to 256×256 and normalized to [-1, 1].
  2. 02The U-Net generator encodes it down to a 1×1×512 bottleneck, then decodes it back up using skip connections from every encoder stage.
  3. 03The PatchGAN discriminator scores 900 overlapping 70×70 patches across the result — each cell below is one of its verdicts.
  4. 04During training, G is pushed to maximize these scores while staying within λ·L1 of the ground truth; D is pushed to call it correctly.
